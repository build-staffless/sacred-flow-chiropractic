About Our Care

Meet Dr. Kelsey

At Sacred Flow Chiropractic & Wellness, care is rooted in connection, presence, and a deep respect for the body's innate intelligence. Rather than chasing symptoms, we focus on regulating the nervous system, the foundation of healing, so the body can shift out of stress and into a state where true repair and transformation can occur. Each session is intentionally designed, blending gentle chiropractic care, Network Spinal, somatic integration, and supportive therapies to help release stored tension, restore flow, and reconnect you to your body's natural rhythm.

Dr. Kelsey's approach is both clinical and deeply human, creating a space where patients feel seen, safe, and supported in their healing process. She works with women, families, and individuals navigating everything from chronic pain and stress to pregnancy and pediatric care, guiding them back to a more regulated, connected, and resilient state. This is not quick-fix care. It's a partnership in healing, where your body is met exactly where it is and supported in moving toward greater ease, vitality, and alignment.

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Sacred Flow different?

We focus on the nervous system as the foundation of health and healing. Care is gentle, specific, and designed to support regulation, adaptability, and long-term function, rather than chasing symptoms or short-term fixes.

Who do you typically work with?

We support women and families navigating chronic stress, pain, autoimmune conditions, hormonal imbalance, fatigue, and nervous system dysregulation. Many of our patients come in after trying multiple approaches and are ready for a more structured, root-cause model of care.

Do I need to be in pain to come in?

No. Many patients seek care to improve regulation, resilience, and overall function rather than address acute pain. Symptoms are one expression of how the nervous system is adapting to stress.

What is the Autonomic Regulation Method™?

A structured approach to care designed to stabilize the nervous system, reduce inflammatory patterns, and restore the body's ability to adapt and heal. It helps shift the body out of chronic stress patterns into a more regulated, resilient state.

What does a visit look like?

Each visit is individualized and may include gentle chiropractic care, Network Spinal, somatic integration techniques, and supportive therapeutic modalities based on your body's needs that day.

How quickly will I see results?

Most patients begin noticing early shifts in sleep, stress tolerance, energy, or symptom patterns within the first several weeks. This work is progressive and designed to create lasting change over time.
